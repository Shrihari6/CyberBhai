Quick Safety Checklist

For Everyone

  • Use a password manager & enable 2FA
  • Think before you click (verify the domain)
  • Keep OS and apps updated
  • Limit public info and app permissions

Admins

  • Enforce EDR, patching, and disk encryption
  • SSH keys only, disable password auth
  • Backups 3-2-1 with restore tests
  • Threat intel & phishing simulations
